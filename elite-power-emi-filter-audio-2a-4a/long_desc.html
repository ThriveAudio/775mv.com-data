This top quality, high efficiency filter is a perfect purifier of the power supplied to the high-end audio equipment.

The filtering methodology and schematics have been approved by hundreds of audiophiles who have been using filters by MyElectrons since 2014. This is the third, improved generation of Power Line Filters for Audio by MyElectrons and Thrive!Audio.

Unrivaled level of EMI / noise suppression for high current power lines
The filter deploys common mode transformers of a very high inductance available on the market for the specified load current. This ensures high efficiency of the common-mode EMI suppression.
Two high inductance differential EMI filtering coils, combined with top grade metal film polypropylene capacitors form a very efficient barrier for the differential EMI / noise.
Careful dampening of both common-mode and differential filtering circuits ensures resonance-free, quiet operation of the filter.

Ground Loop braking is more efficient and safer than found in most other commercially available Hum Suppressors / Eliminators.
Use of very powerful diodes provides for a safe system free of ground wire loops. Careful dampening of the ground loop breaker prevents any resonance effects. Current handling capacity of the grounding circuit is rated at more than 250A for 1s.

Zero Noise Overvoltage Protection
Overvoltage protection is applied between the load and neutral lines. Both lines are fused.
